Privacy Policy for SortSatisfy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

SortSatisfy ("we", "us", "our") is a mobile game published by Sanket Bodhare. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the SortSatisfy mobile application (the "App"), how that information is used, and your choices regarding it.

Information the App Collects Directly

The App does not collect personally identifiable information directly. All game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device in a save file. We do not maintain server-side accounts or profiles.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with its own privacy practices:

  • Google Play Services. Google's standard platform services for Android apps. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Google AdMob. We use AdMob to display rewarded video advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect your Android Advertising ID and information about your interaction with ads. See AdMob's data disclosures.

Advertising

The App displays rewarded video advertisements served by Google AdMob. These ads are optional. They appear only when you actively tap a "Watch Ad" button within the App to earn in-game rewards. We do not display interstitial or banner ads that interrupt gameplay.

AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID to show personalized ads. You control this through your device settings:

  • To opt out of personalized ads: open your Android Settings, go to Google, then Ads, then toggle Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • To reset your Advertising ID at any time: same settings screen, tap Reset advertising ID.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at sortsatisfy@gmail.com so that we can investigate and take appropriate action.

Data Storage and Deletion

Your game data is stored locally on your device. To delete all data associated with SortSatisfy, uninstall the App from your device. We do not retain server-side copies of your game progress or personal information.

If cloud save functionality is added in a future version of the App, this Privacy Policy will be updated to describe what data is synchronized and how you can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect changes in the App or in applicable law. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
