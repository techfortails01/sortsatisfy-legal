Privacy Policy for SortSatisfy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

SortSatisfy ("we", "us", "our") is a mobile game published by Sanket Bodhare. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the SortSatisfy mobile application (the "App"), how that information is used, and your choices regarding it.

Information the App Collects Directly

The App does not collect personally identifiable information directly. All game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device in a save file. We do not maintain server-side accounts or profiles.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with its own privacy practices:

  • Google Play Services. Google's standard platform services for Android apps. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Google AdMob. We use AdMob to display rewarded video advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect your Android Advertising ID and information about your interaction with ads. See AdMob's data disclosures.

Your Data Rights and How to Delete Your Data

You have the right to delete your data at any time. SortSatisfy stores most of your data locally on your device, so deletion is straightforward.

Local Game Data (Progress, Settings, Coins)

All your game progress, settings, earned coins, daily streak, and preferences are stored only on your device. To delete this data, choose either option:

  • Uninstall the App: open your device's app settings, find SortSatisfy, and tap Uninstall. All local game data is deleted immediately.
  • Clear App Data: open Android Settings, go to Apps, tap SortSatisfy, then Storage, then Clear Data. Your save file is deleted but the App remains installed.

Advertising Data (AdMob)

AdMob may store your Android Advertising ID and information about ads you have interacted with. To delete or reset this data:

  • Reset your Advertising ID: open Android Settings, go to Privacy or Google, tap Ads, then tap Reset advertising ID.
  • Opt out of personalized ads: in the same Ads settings screen, toggle Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • Manage your Google Ad Settings: visit adssettings.google.com to view and delete data Google has collected for advertising purposes.

Other Requests or Questions

If you have any other data deletion requests, privacy questions, or concerns that are not addressed above, contact us by email:

Email: sortsatisfy@gmail.com

We will respond to data deletion requests within 30 days. Please include your device type and a brief description of your request so that we can help you efficiently.

Advertising

The App displays rewarded video advertisements served by Google AdMob. These ads are optional. They appear only when you actively tap a "Watch Ad" button within the App to earn in-game rewards. We do not display interstitial or banner ads that interrupt gameplay.

AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID to show personalized ads. You control this through your device settings, as described in the data deletion section above.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at sortsatisfy@gmail.com so that we can investigate and take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect changes in the App or in applicable law. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
